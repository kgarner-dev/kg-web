Stencled is a purpose-built business platform for independent tattoo artists and piercers. Most artists are running their businesses on Instagram DMs, paper consent forms, and tools that were never designed for them — Stencled replaces all of it with a back-office dashboard and a live public microsite, connected in real time. Every booking triggers a fully automated client pipeline from confirmation through aftercare, every module is toggleable to fit any artist's workflow, and the platform is built on one non-negotiable principle: artists keep 100% of what they earn.
